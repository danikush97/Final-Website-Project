Interesting Facts About the Park :

  • Zion is a Hebrew word that means 'a place of peace and relaxation.' This was the name given to the canyon in the 1860s by Mormon pioneers.
  • The park encompasses 150k acres of land.
  • It protects several ancient archeological sites
  • Home to one of the most dangerous hikes in the world: 'Angel's Landing.'
  • Known for good climbing
  • It is still growing.
  • About 2.5m people visit the park each year.
